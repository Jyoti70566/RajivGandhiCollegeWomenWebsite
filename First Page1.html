Here you will get information about Rajiv Gandhi Government College For Women(Bhiwani)


Rajiv Gandhi Government College For Women, Bhiwani was established in 2003.Now the
college has been known as Rajiv Gandhi Govt. College For Women.

The institution is affiliated to C.B.L.U. University,Bhiwani, recognized by U.G.C.,New Delhi.
In the beginning 204 students were admitted to the college in Art Faculty. Considering the good Results,
commerce faculty was also granted.

It is known to everbody that college has not its own building and it is running in the building of S.I.E.M.T. Despite this in the short span of mere six years the college marks its own place.

It is a matter of pride that the small plant sown mere eighteen year before grows so fast that it turns into a tree and nominated in the list of college of excellence by the govt. of Haryana.
